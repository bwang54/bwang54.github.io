Beyond the standard undergraduate curriculum, I am actively involved in educational outreach designed to introduce younger students to the mechanics of the financial system.

### Kean University Bloomberg Education Summer Pre-College Program
**Term:** Summer 2025

I designed and instructed a core module tailored specifically for high-achieving high school students. The program provides an intensive, hands-on introduction to:
* Fundamentals of global financial markets
* Principles of entrepreneurship finance
* Practical navigation and data retrieval using the Bloomberg Terminal

This initiative helps demystify the financial sector for prospective university students and provides them with early exposure to industry-standard analytical tools.
