As the financial industry increasingly relies on big data and algorithmic analysis, it is critical to bridge the gap between traditional financial theory and modern programming capabilities.

### FIN-3390: Data Analytics in Finance
**Status:** Launched Fall 2025

I am spearheading the creation and launch of this new course designed to equip students with cutting-edge **Python** skills applied directly to financial analysis. 

The curriculum moves beyond standard spreadsheet modeling, introducing students to programmatic data retrieval, econometrics modeling, and data visualization. By integrating Python into the finance workflow, students are prepared for the rigorous quantitative demands of modern financial institutions.
