As part of my commitment to providing students with practical, industry-standard skills, I designed and lead a comprehensive Bloomberg Crash Course in April 2026. This module introduces students to real-world financial data, bridging the gap between academic theory and professional application.

### Course Highlights

* **Certification Preparation:** Guides students through the foundational knowledge required for the Bloomberg Finance Fundamentals (BFF) and Bloomberg Market Concepts (BMC) credentials.
* **Terminal Navigation:** Covers essential command syntax, specialized keyboard logic, and efficient data retrieval techniques.
* **Fundamental Equity Analysis:** Provides hands-on training for extracting corporate financials, consensus earnings estimates, and advanced valuation inputs.
* **Macroeconomic & Fund Analysis:** Introduces global market dashboards, economic indicator tracking, and Exchange-Traded Fund (ETF) screening methodologies.

This intensive module ensures students are well-prepared for the quantitative demands of modern financial institutions and advanced academic research.
